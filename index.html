Project snapshot

Clear problem, clear solution, clear moat.

Problem

Rural communities face talent hollowing and limited STEAM/aesthetic resources, while traditional willow weaving risks “aging artisans & fading inheritance”.

Solution

A closed loop of education empowerment → digital co‑creation → commercialization, transforming heritage craftsmanship into sustainable income and cultural pride.

Flagship product

Modern, eco‑friendly willow‑woven pet homes (cat/dog beds) redesigned for comfort, aesthetics, and story‑rich branding.

Core technology: “ZhiShe · Artisan Space”

  • Web‑based 3D interaction (low barrier; mobile‑friendly).
  • Co‑creation: coloring/pattern recomposition for product models.
  • Design outputs become reusable digital assets and can connect to production guidance.

Use this as your “technical moat” section in the video.

Education empowerment methodology (4 steps)

  1. Learn in the village: immerse & diagnose needs.
  2. Learn from the village: master artisans’ weaving logic.
  3. Learn for the village: redesign with digital tools & industrial design.
  4. Let the village learn: leave courses/tools for long‑term support.
